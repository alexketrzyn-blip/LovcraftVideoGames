# README – Part of Master's Thesis

##  Overview
This file is a part of my master's thesis.  
The thesis explored the works of **H.P. Lovecraft** and his followers, as well as the cultural impact of his prose in literature, music, film, and video games.

---

##  Previous Chapters (not included in this repository)
- **Chapter 1**: Analysis of Lovecraft’s short stories and the works of his imitators.  
- **Chapter 2**: Descriptions and reviews of song lyrics and films based on Lovecraft’s prose.   
  - Examined fragments of song lyrics and connected them to Lovecraft’s stories.  

---

##  Chapter 3 – Video Games Inspired by Lovecraft
This chapter, included in the repository, focused on **video games inspired by Lovecraftian motifs**.  
Titles analyzed:  
- *Alone in the Dark I & II*  
- *Prisoner of Ice*  
- *Shadow of the Comet*  
- *Necronomicon: The Dawning of Darkness*  
- *Neverwinter Nights*  
- *Quake*
- *Call of Cthulhu: Dark Corners of the Earth*

The research required **hours of gameplay**, analysis of Lovecraftian themes, and the preparation of **reviews and player instructions**.
